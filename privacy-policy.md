# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 2026

VoicePDFMemo is a business productivity application designed to create, edit, and manage customer contracts.

## Information Used

VoicePDFMemo may request access to:

- Microphone (for voice dictation)
- Speech Recognition
- iCloud (CloudKit) to synchronize contracts across the user's devices
- User-created documents and signatures

## Data Collection

VoicePDFMemo does not collect, sell, or share personal information for advertising or analytics purposes.

All contract information is created by the user and remains under the user's control. If iCloud Sync is enabled, contract data is securely synchronized using Apple's CloudKit services associated with the user's Apple ID.
